OUR ESSENTIAL MATERIALS

Iron

Silver-gray metal with magnetic properties.

  • High hardness and density

  • It has different structural forms

  • Fundamental in steel products

Our Java 4 mine is located 110 km from the Lázaro Cárdenas port, comprising a total of 11,169.86 hectares.
Adjacent to Division 8 is the mine owned by Arcelor Mittal, its reserves have been tested, are reviewed annually and are part of the market valuation of Arcelor's shares.
In the vicinity is also the mine called Doña Paula, located south of Division 1, which is currently producing and exporting around 150,000 MT per month.

OUR ESSENTIAL MATERIALS

Antimony

This semimetallic element has four allotropic forms. In its stable form it is a bluish-white metal.

  • It has a low electrical conductivity

  • Chemical behavior as a nonmetal

  • It is calcophilic, appearing with sulfur and other elements

Our main mine is strategically located in Real de Catorce, San Luis Potosí, less than 20 km from the “Estación Wadley” freight train station that connects to the port of Manzanillo on the Pacific coast of Mexico.
The mine has been in operation for more than 100 years, it resumed full scale mining operation 12 years ago.
We have a lease of the mine from SB Wadley mining company, a large mining group focused on the exploitation and marketing of Gold, Silver and Copper.

OUR ESSENTIAL MATERIALS

Rhodium

Silver-white ductile metal

  • Melting point higher than that of platinum

  • It is a subsidiary of other mining activities

  • It is considered the most expensive metal in the world.

  • Used as a catalyst for hydrogenation

Our mine is located in the municipality of Purepero in the state of Michoacán, 5 hours from the port of Lázaro Cárdenas. The infrastructure and extraction processes at the site provide for the production of at least 100 to 200 MT per month of high content rhodium clay.
